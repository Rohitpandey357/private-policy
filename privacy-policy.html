Arcade Atlas

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Arcade Atlas is designed to work offline and does not require an account. The app stores game settings, favorites, recent plays, achievements, and local records on the device so the arcade can remember progress between sessions.

Offline-first experience
No account required
Local device storage for progress and settings

Information We Collect

Arcade Atlas does not ask users to create an account or submit personal information inside the app. The app stores gameplay preferences, favorites, recent activity, achievements, and local high scores on the device using local storage.

How We Use Information

Stored local data is used only to power app features such as saved shelves, game settings, onboarding state, achievements, and records. This information is used only to improve the in-app experience on that device.

Data Sharing and Transfers

Arcade Atlas does not send personal information to a remote server, does not include in-app chat, and does not intentionally share user data with advertisers or data brokers. Platform providers such as Apple or Google may collect technical information through their own store or device services under their own privacy terms.

Children and Families

Arcade Atlas is built as a family-friendly offline games hub. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents and guardians should review platform-level privacy controls available on their devices and app store accounts.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because Arcade Atlas stores gameplay data locally on the device, that data generally remains until the app is removed or the app storage is cleared by the user through device settings.

Security

Arcade Atlas uses the storage and security controls provided by the operating system and Expo runtime. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but the app is designed to minimize risk by avoiding unnecessary personal data collection.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if Arcade Atlas adds new features, online services, or store-related requirements. When the policy changes, the updated date will be revised in the app and in the published policy document.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the official Arcade Atlas store page or publisher website that distributes the app.
